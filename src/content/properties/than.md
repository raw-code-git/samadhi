Than is a small room — twelve tables — on the cliff edge at Japamala. The kitchen cooks what the island provides: line-caught fish from that morning's boats, coconuts and pandan from the property's own garden, *sambal* made fresh each service.

The style is modern Asian without the gimmick. Dishes are plated with restraint; the view does most of the work. Sunset seating books out first — reserve two days ahead if you can.
