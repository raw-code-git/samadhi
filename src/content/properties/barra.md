Barra is the easy one. A thatched roof, a long counter, a row of loungers on the sand. The grill and the wood-fired oven run from lunchtime onwards; the bar opens with breakfast and closes when the last table leaves.

The menu is short and coastal: fish tacos, pizzas, a daily ceviche, a few salads from the kitchen garden. Drinks are strong and cold. If you want something more composed, Than is five minutes uphill.
