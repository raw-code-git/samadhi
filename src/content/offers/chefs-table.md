The counter at FOOK seats six. The chefs work a metre away — shaping, folding, fanning the fire — and the menu shifts with what walked in that morning from the market.

Seven courses, built around the wood-fired oven and the steamer, paired with a short flight of Chinese teas. Come hungry, come early, and plan to stay for the last course.

Pairs well with a night at Villa Samadhi — add the stay at the booking step.
