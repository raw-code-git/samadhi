Tioman's southern reefs are among the most intact in the South China Sea, and the dive season from March through October is long, warm and reliably calm.

Our dive week pairs accommodation at Japamala with ten guided dives across the best of the island's sites: Tiger Reef, Bahara Rock, the wrecks at Soyak. Evenings are yours: a cocktail at Barra on the sand, or a longer dinner at Than as the light fades.

Our in-house marine biologist gives a short orientation on day one — a look at what you're seeing down there, and why it matters.
